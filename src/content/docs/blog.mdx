## Recent

- [**Every $20 AI subscription costs about $100 to serve. The bill is coming.**](/blog/ai-subscription-bomb/), May 2026.
  AI labs are running an industry-wide loss-leader. Anthropic users
  burn $8 in compute per $1 of subscription revenue. Microsoft was
  losing $20+ on every GitHub Copilot seat. Agents broke the math,
  and Copilot moves to usage-based billing on June 1.

- [**Mullvad gave you 8 trillion exit IPs. 9 servers found you.**](/blog/mullvad-vpn-fingerprint/), May 2026.
  A researcher rotated through 3,650 WireGuard keys against a VPN
  that advertises millions of possible exit-IP combinations, and saw
  only 284. The reason is a single seeded float used to pick an
  index in every server's pool.

- [**AI slop killed the open-source bug bounty**](/blog/ai-slop-killed-the-bug-bounty/), May 2026.
  A real database project just shut down a $1,000-per-bug program after
  AI agents flooded the queue with fictional vulnerabilities. The cost
  isn't tokens. It's maintainer attention.
